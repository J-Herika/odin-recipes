Dount Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 2¼ teaspoons (one package) active dry yeast
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) butter, melted and cooled
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1teaspoon salt
  • 4¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling out the dough
  • 2quarts neutral oil, for frying, plus more for the bowl.

Step 1: Prepare Dough

Warm the milk until around 90 degrees,

  • then mix with yeast until foamy (about 5 mins).

Step 2: Prepare Dough Mixture

  • Combine eggs, butter, sugar, and salt into the yeast mix.
  • Add half the flour, mix until combined, then add the rest. Adjust flour if too wet.
  • Knead dough until smooth, let rise for about 1 hour.

Step 3: Shape Dough

  • Roll dough to ½-inch thickness, cut out doughnuts, reserving holes
  • Let dough scraps rest, then repeat the process.

Step 4: Rise Again

  • Place doughnuts on floured sheets, let rise for about 45 minutes.
